US Commercial Flights - 2014

The Research and Technology Bureau of Transportation Statistics (RITA) tracks commercial airline flight summary information. These visualizations represent arrivals and departures of 2014 commercial flights with origin and destination in the continental United States.

This chart represents the 50 busiest airports by flight volume. These airports are considered airline hubs, meaning that these airports may be used as transfer points to the passengers' intended destinations.
